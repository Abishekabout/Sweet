Our Story

About Aahhaa
Palkova

Aahhaa Palkova is a well-loved bakery and sweet shop located on Salem Main Road in Kaveripattinam, Tamil Nadu. Known for serving authentic Tamil sweets like Palkova, Adhirasam, and a wide range of snacks and beverages.

A must-stop for travellers on the Salem–Bangalore highway. Whether you need a strong cup of black tea or a box of fresh palkova, we're open daily until 10 PM — ready to serve you.

Our Specialities

Made Fresh, Every Day

Pure ingredients, time-honoured recipes — the flavours you grew up loving, still made the same way.

🍮

Palkova

Our signature slow-cooked milk sweet — rich, creamy, and melt-in-your-mouth. The reason everyone stops here.

🍩

Adhirasam

Crispy, golden jaggery rice dumplings. A traveller favourite on the Salem–Bangalore highway — one of the best you'll ever have.

🌀

Murukku & Pabila

Crunchy murukku, pabila, and more — the perfect snack to pair with our bold, aromatic black tea.

🍵

Special Black Tea

Bold, strong, aromatic black tea in many varieties — the reason regulars stop here on every single trip.

🥛

Milk & Curd

Farm-fresh milk and curd sourced locally — pure, wholesome, and naturally refreshing every time.

✨

Ghee Sweets

Traditional ghee-based sweets crafted for festivals, celebrations, and sweet everyday indulgence.
